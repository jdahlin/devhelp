Testing the dialog

You can test your dialog by pressing the "Test" toolbar icon. If you wish to test your dialog in a "live" environment, that is by actually connecting to GConf and changing GConf keys, press the "Test (gconf)" icon.

If you test without GConf, the "Sensitivities" you set up above will not work, as those depend on GConf.
